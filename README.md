### Key Insights
- United States & India dominate content production
- TV-MA (mature) is the most common rating
- Documentaries, Stand-Up Comedy & International Movies lead genres
- Movies: 68.42% | TV Shows: 31.58%
- Sharp growth in Netflix originals post-2016
